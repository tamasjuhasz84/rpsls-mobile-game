# RPSLS User Guide (HU/EN)

Datum: 2026-03-31
Statusz: Finalized (Day 17)

## English

### 1. Getting started

1. Open the Home screen.
2. Enter your player name.
3. Choose a mode: BO3, BO5, or Survival.
4. Tap Start for a new run, or Continue if a valid saved run is available.

### 2. Home panels

- Daily Challenge: one seeded challenge per day.
- Missions: complete objectives and claim rewards.
- Stats: track wins, losses, draws, and streaks.
- Leaderboard: local daily and all-time ranking.

### 3. During a match

1. Pick your move before countdown ends.
2. Wait for reveal and round result.
3. Continue until one side reaches the mode target.
4. After a win, use Advance to fight the next opponent.

### 4. Modes

- BO3: first to 3 round wins.
- BO5: first to 5 round wins.
- Survival: endless opponent progression with score tracking.
- Daily Challenge: one fixed daily run with completion state.

### 5. Data and privacy

- The game stores progress locally on your device.
- Main legal pages are available in-app at `/privacy` and `/terms`.

### 6. Product principles

- No ads
- No paid advantage
- No pay-to-win mechanics

## Magyar

### 1. Kezdes

1. Nyisd meg a Home kepernyot.
2. Add meg a jatekosnevedet.
3. Valassz modot: BO3, BO5 vagy Survival.
4. Uj futashoz nyomd meg a Start gombot, vagy hasznald a Continue-t, ha van ervenyes mentett allapot.

### 2. Home panelek

- Daily Challenge: napi egy seedelt kihivas.
- Missions: teljesitheto celok es begyujtheto jutalmak.
- Stats: gyozelem/vereseg/dontetlen es sorozat statisztikak.
- Leaderboard: lokal napi es orokranglista.

### 3. Meccs kozben

1. A visszaszamlalas vege elott valaszd ki a lepesedet.
2. Varj a felfedesre es a koreredmenyre.
3. Addig megy a meccs, amig valamelyik fel eleri a mod celjat.
4. Gyozelem utan az Advance gombbal lephetsz a kovetkezo ellenfelre.

### 4. Modok

- BO3: elso 3 korgyozelemig.
- BO5: elso 5 korgyozelemig.
- Survival: vegtelen ellenfel-sor, pontszamkovetessel.
- Daily Challenge: napi fix futas, napi teljesitesi allapottal.

### 5. Adatkezeles roviden

- A jatek a haladast helyben, a keszuleken tarolja.
- A jogi oldalak az appban a `/privacy` es `/terms` utvonalon erhetoek el.

### 6. Termekelvek

- Nincs reklam
- Nincs fizetos elony
- Nincs pay-to-win mechanika
